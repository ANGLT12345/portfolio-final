About me

Hi, I'm Liang Tze, a changemaker driven by a passion for leveraging technology, education, and creativity to inspire meaningful change. As the Founder of Kids Learn Code, I've had the privilege of empowering over 100 students, including underprivileged youth, by teaching them coding skills and fostering innovation. Watching students develop confidence and creativity fuels my commitment to building initiatives that encourage others to dream big. At SST, I've honed my expertise in project management, team leadership, and creative problem-solving. My journey is marked by notable achievements, including being a Top 10 Finalist in Samsung Solve for Tomorrow 2023, winning the Design Excellence Award at ARTC, and receiving recognition in STEM and environmental competitions. Beyond academics, I actively contribute to my community through volunteering with organizations like the National Library Board. I was also honored to be shortlisted for the 2024 President's Volunteer and Philanthropy Award. My professional experiences extend to impactful roles such as leading tech deployments during my internship at Brahm Centre Ltd and organizing workshops for Kids Learn Code. These opportunities have allowed me to blend technical expertise with a collaborative spirit to drive innovation and social impact.
