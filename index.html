About Me

Hi, I'm Liang Tze, a changemaker driven by a passion for leveraging technology, education, and creativity to inspire meaningful change. As the Founder of Kids Learn Code, I've had the privilege of empowering over 400 students and volunteers, including underprivileged youth, by teaching them coding skills and fostering innovation.

At just 16 years old, I'm currently studying at School of Science and Technology Singapore while leading initiatives that make quality coding education accessible to every child, regardless of their background.
